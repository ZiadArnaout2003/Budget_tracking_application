Is it mandatory to give us this access to benefit from TYB?

We have built this automated system to make it as user-friendly and beneficial as possible. Most people don't have the time to input every transaction conducted during a day. That's why we take the responsibility to do it for the user, so they can enjoy our services without worrying about anything.

That said, it can be very useful to manually enter the bill, the location of the transaction, and the spending category. This helps us better analyze and understand your spending distribution, allowing us to provide more customized advice.
